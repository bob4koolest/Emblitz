Game Tutorial

Thanks for playing Emblitz! Emblitz is an online fast-paced real time strategy game. Players can battle each other out on maps. Some maps include scale real-world places for you to fight over! The game mechanics are simple, but there's a lot of strategy involved. Good luck, and have fun playing!

Objectives

The main objective of Emblitz is to conquor the world by taking over every single territory on the map.


Controls

To start off the controls list first we must discuss the menu controls. Before we get to the menu items you have some options to customize yourself for when you join the game such as the username box for how your name will be displayed in game and the color selector for which color you prefer. After this we have the menu control options which are current map selection, play, join game, and create room. The 'Play' button will create a public room meaning that anybody can join your game or it will join you into an already exsisting public room, the 'Create Room' button in contrast creates a private room meaning that only people who you give your room code to can join the game (room codes are labeled as Room Join Code and appear after you press the 'Play' or 'Create Room' button). You can join your friends rooms by pasting the room code of the game which you want to join in the text box which appers when you click the 'Join Room' button then clicking connect.

When you get into the game the main control sceme is to click a territory then click a territory which you want to move troops to weather this be to fortify or attack another territory. You can use your slider at the bottom to decide what percentage of troops you want to move from the first territory to the next.


Game Mechanics

The main mechanic in Emblitz is warfare. This mainly includes attack and defense which you can use by keeping troops in your territories to fortify and moving troops into neutral and enemy territories to attack.

Attacking can be extremely useful for taking chunks of land from your enemy to get a greater amount of total troops per turn as well as inhibiting your enemy as each territory generates 20% + 1 more troops than it already had. There is a cap of 5 troops per territory per troop addition. This makes conquering vast swaths of territory incredibly useful for growing an army and crushing your enemies.

Defending and fortifying can aso be extremely helpful as not only are you putting up a fight when someone attacks you but you are also getting a 20% home-field advantage against your attacker.
